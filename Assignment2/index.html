Our Products

Our Ingredients

We love to experiment with new ingredients and scent blends and our range is constantly changing, just like our mood! Our soaps and uniquely scented bath and body products are handmade entirely from scratch to enhance your skin. Our soaps are definitely our stars.

As our skin is bombarded daily with bacteria and dirt, it is important to use our soaps to preserve the health of our skin for a better and longer life. Our soaps are made with essential oils, which have been used for thousands of years in various cultures for medicinal and health purposes. This is why our soaps are number one in New Zealand.
