Our Products

At Soap Co. we believe that our creations, like any other produce made with natural ingredients should be enjoyed while they are at their freshest.

This is why you can only get your soaps directly from us in store at Soap Co. Our products are made, disaplyed and sold straight out of our kitchen (by the same two hands, no less)

If our soaps are not sold within a week, the remainder is donated to charity.
