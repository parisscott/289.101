Who Are We?

Chaos is a fashion label that is inspired by trends around the world, combining artistic elements with textile techniques, allowing clothing to be transformed from nothing to something. The name ‘Chaos’ comes from the artistic elements that have been added to the clothing to make them wearable. The most popular clothing that Chaos makes revolves around denim. Denim jackets, denim jeans, classic tees, pinned dresses and belts to complete what is known as the 'classic look.' Chaos fashion label was created so that clothing which is thrown away and can no longer be worn is transformed into a piece of art that can be showcased on the human body. Chaos revolves around taking the unusable and transforming it into the usable, for people who value one off contemporary fashion, with an element of chic, grunge fashion. Chaos explores issues that relate to sustainabilty, body image and the way females view themselves, so that the importance of fashion in the form of self-expression is highlighted among those who purchase clothing from Chaos fashion label. Chaos hopes to create a new sense of style that is unique and reflects what the fashion label believes in. This will allow the fashion label to create something that has never been done before; high end upcycled fashion that transforms clothing from nothing to something.
